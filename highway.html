Project Overview

The National Highway Project aims to improve transportation infrastructure by constructing a modern, multi-lane highway system. The project will enhance connectivity between major cities, reduce travel time, and support economic development.

Key Features

  • Four-lane divided highway
  • Modern safety features and lighting
  • Toll plazas with electronic payment
  • Rest areas and emergency services
  • Eco-friendly design with minimal environmental impact
